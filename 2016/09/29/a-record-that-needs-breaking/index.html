A record that needs breaking

It's been about 5 years since I first stumbled across the Human Powered Land Speed [World] Record, and the competition for the record is certainly stiff. It just got broken a couple weeks ago with a run of 89MPH!!

Looking through the records, I had originally noticed that the tandem record is slower than the single person record...and since top speed is simply a matter of power overcoming drag, it should be faster!
