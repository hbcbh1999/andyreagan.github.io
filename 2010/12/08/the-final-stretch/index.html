The final stretch

Wed 08 December 2010

Classes have ended for the fall of 2010, and I'm nearly halfway through my third year of college.  It's hard to believe that it's December, but I realize pretty quick when I step outside to 20F and wind.  This semester has been an absolute blast, and now I'm beginning to prepare for all that stands between myself and home in NY: finals.

I don't really have that much to say, I'll be studying hard for my three finals after Bike Rider's Ball tonight!  Coming home Dec 17th, with a car full of people that I picked up via VT's Rideboard, so I'll have company and gas money for the ride back, not to mention all of those carbon emissions that we'll be saving!
