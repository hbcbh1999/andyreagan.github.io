Sept 19th

Sun 19 September 2010

Today was a second cross country race, and I really enjoyed this one.  Didn’t crash, and was able to ride hard which was fun.  Started out in first but after a mile my chain dropped and the train passed me, but I kept pushing and passed five of them for 5th place by the end.  I think that a longer race would have favored me, really but it was good.

The ride back was long, but I rode with Ritchie and drove the last two hours so it was a good ride.  Hangin out at the tri house for a little while, Alex Brown let me borrow his wetsuit!! And I got a number belt for the run at Big Lick…so I have got all the gear for sure. and I’m pumped.
