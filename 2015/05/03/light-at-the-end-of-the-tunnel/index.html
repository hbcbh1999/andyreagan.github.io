Light at the end of the tunnel

Sun 03 May 2015

With the semester nearing an end (one final exam taken, two presentations to give next week)...professor Bagrow sums up how I feel:

Is there a thing like jet lag but for the semester? Feel like I could sleep for a month!

— Jim Bagrow (@bagrow) May 1, 2015

A random subselection of highlights over the past two months:

  • Sam's business, Wildflower Studios has secured and loan, a space, a web designer (guess who), and will be opening in September
  • a self-declared win at the student research conference
  • a few excellent bike rides including a crit race, and an attempt at the 6 gap ride
  • and lots of science

And looking forward to presentating at the 2015 SIAM Conference on Dynamical Systems, weddings, research, and bike riding this summer!
