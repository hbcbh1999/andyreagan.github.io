Strava art

Wed 15 January 2020

As a passionate both data visualization practitioner and amateur fitness enthusiast, my hobbies have neat intersection in fitness data. I enjoy using Strava to keep track of running, cycling, and all nature of outdoor exploits, and it just so happens that the data is really fun to make visualizations with. I was inspired by the for-purchase data art at Sisu:
