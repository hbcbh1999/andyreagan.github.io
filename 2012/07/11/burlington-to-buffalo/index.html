Burlington to Buffalo

Wed 11 July 2012

From the new house in Burlington, Sam and I headed out on a crazy and awesome trip on our bikes. We joined with Bike and Build's Northern US route for 5 of the 7 days, doing our own things for the first and last segments. Click on the above map for the Garmin data.

Sam was riding Mr. Bikey, now certified at 9.5cm of DROP. I measured in at 14.5cm on Kazane, not too shabby either. Here's the twitter rundown.
