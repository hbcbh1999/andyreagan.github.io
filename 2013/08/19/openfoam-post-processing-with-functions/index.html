OpenFOAM post-processing with functions

Mon 19 August 2013

I had to really search to find examples of using the built-in utilities in OpenFOAM to post-process while running a case.

In my case, I want to output the mass flow rate through a cross-sectional patch in my simulation. Anyway, find all of the examples with:

find \$FOAM_TUTORIALS -name controlDict | xargs grep -A 25 functions
